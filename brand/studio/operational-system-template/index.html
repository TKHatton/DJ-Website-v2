What it does

A service business runs on a hundred small loops.

Lead in, scored, nurtured. Client onboarded, served, billed, offboarded. Content drafted, reviewed, published. Money tracked, owed, collected. Each loop has rules, exceptions, handoffs. Most teams run them out of someone's head.

The Operational System Template is one engine that runs all of it. Six industry playbooks ship in the box. The engine itself works for any service business that runs on repeated workflows, configured once, kept current as the business changes.

03 / How it works

Four phases. You stay in the seat for decisions.

  1. Configure. We set up your voice, brand, services, and one industry playbook. One to two weeks.
  2. Test. Run your real workflows through the system. We refine in place. Nothing goes live until it earns it.
  3. Deploy. The engine takes over the operational loops. You stay in the seat for decisions, judgment calls, and exceptions.
  4. Operate. Daily commands run the work. The engine adapts as your business changes. Updates ship for the life of the license.

A note on agent design

Five things that work beat forty that demo well. The system runs on capability-scoped agents, not single-task ones. Better infrastructure, fewer agents, more reliable outcomes.
