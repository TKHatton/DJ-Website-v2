What it does

Email in. Spreadsheet out.

Tell InboxToSheet which Gmail label or query to watch. Tell it which Google Sheet and tab to write to. It handles the rest. Sender, subject, date, body, attachments. Mapped into the columns you want.

No Zapier subscription. No "API access" setup. No code. You connect your accounts once and it runs.

02 / What's included

Everything in the box.

  • Gmail-to-Sheets pipeline. Set it once and forget it.
  • Filter by label, sender, subject, or query.
  • Custom column mapping. Pick what fields go where.
  • Body parsing for structured emails (invoices, receipts, leads).
  • Attachment handling. Save links or download.
  • Run on demand or on a schedule.

Real estate agents tracking lead emails. Bookkeepers pulling invoice receipts. Sales teams who live in their inbox. Operations folks who want a clean activity log. Anyone running a business from Gmail and wishing the data was somewhere they could actually look at it.
