03 / How it works

Four phases. You stay in control.

  1. Set up. First-launch wizard creates your business profile, uploads your logo, sets your defaults. Two minutes.
  2. Invoice. Pick a client from autocomplete. Line items pre-fill from the last invoice. Preview renders live. Generate the PDF. Send.
  3. Track. Every invoice logs automatically. Mark paid from anywhere. Overdue flags itself. Forecast updates from your real payment patterns.
  4. Export. One click at year-end gives your accountant a clean PDF and CSV. Tax time is two minutes, not two days.

A note on the AI agent

The agent is optional. The app works completely without it. When enabled, it never sends anything without your review. Local providers (Ollama, LM Studio) keep everything on your machine. Cloud providers (Gemini, OpenAI, Claude) send only the specific query, never your full database. You bring your own key. We never touch your billing.
