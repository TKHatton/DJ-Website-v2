Real ideas, real submissions. We can't promise we'll build everything we hear, but we read everything that comes in. The good ones make their way into the Studio.

How this works

Tell us what's missing.

The best products started as something we needed and couldn't find. Sometimes that's a tool we built for ourselves. Sometimes it's an idea a stranger sent in.

Send the idea. Be specific about the workflow, who would use it, and what currently makes the work painful. Specifics beat vague.

What we look for

Workflows that repeat. Tools that should exist but don't. Pain that's specific enough to design around. Not "make me a chatbot."
