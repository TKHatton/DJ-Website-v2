02 / Origin

We built this for ourselves first.

Most of what we sell started as something we needed and couldn't find. A way to pull email threads into a sheet without writing code. A file organizer that didn't lock us into a subscription. An operational system that ran without watching it.

When something works for us, we make it work for everyone else. That's the whole posture. We don't ship products we wouldn't use.

"We cut the corners on the right things."
